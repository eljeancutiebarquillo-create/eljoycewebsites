About WV2 Condotel Luxury

Welcome to WV2 Condotel Luxury — where contemporary design meets warm Filipino hospitality. Nestled in a prime Iloilo City location, WV2 is more than a place to stay; it's a destination crafted for travelers who value comfort, convenience, and curated experiences. Our suites combine elegant interiors, thoughtful amenities, and sweeping views that showcase the charm of Iloilo.

Whether you're here for business or leisure, WV2 offers an environment designed to help you recharge and reconnect. Start your morning with a complimentary gourmet breakfast, sip signature cocktails at the SkyView Bar, or cool off at our rooftop infinity pool while watching the sunset over the city skyline. For guests who need to stay productive, we provide high-speed Wi-Fi, dedicated workspaces in select suites, and a quiet executive lounge.

Families will appreciate our safe and fun children’s play area, while couples can book intimate spa treatments and private dining experiences. Our friendly, 24/7 concierge team is ready to assist with reservations, local recommendations, airport transfers, and customized experiences — because at WV2 we believe luxury is in the details.
