About WV2 Condotel Luxury

Welcome to WV2 Condotel Luxury — where contemporary design meets warm Filipino hospitality. Located at the heart of Iloilo City, this modern sanctuary combines elegant interiors, vibrant colors, and luxury amenities perfect for every traveler.

Whether you’re a business guest, a couple on a romantic escape, or a family on vacation, WV2 offers an unforgettable stay. Relax at our rooftop infinity pool, enjoy a drink at the SkyView Bar, or let your kids play in our safe and fun children’s playground. Our dedicated staff ensures every guest feels at home — from check-in to check-out.

Each room is thoughtfully designed with plush bedding, high-speed Wi-Fi, Smart TV, and select units with private balconies overlooking Iloilo’s skyline. WV2 Condotel Luxury is where comfort meets sophistication.
