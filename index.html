About WV2 Condotel Luxury

Welcome to WV2 Condotel Luxury — where contemporary design meets warm Filipino hospitality. Nestled in a prime Iloilo City location, WV2 is more than a place to stay; it's a destination crafted for travelers who value comfort, convenience, and curated experiences.

Whether you're here for business or leisure, WV2 offers an environment designed to help you recharge and reconnect. Start your morning with a complimentary gourmet breakfast, sip signature cocktails at the SkyView Bar, or cool off at our rooftop infinity pool while watching the sunset over the city skyline.
